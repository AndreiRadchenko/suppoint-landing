About Suppoint

We are on a mission to make customer support effortless for teams of every size.

Suppoint was founded by a small team of engineers and support specialists who were frustrated with clunky, overpriced helpdesk tools. We set out to build the product we always wished existed — fast, intuitive, and genuinely pleasant to use every day.

Today Suppoint powers support teams across dozens of industries, from early-stage startups to growing enterprises. Our platform brings ticketing, live chat, and a self-service knowledge base into one unified workspace so nothing falls through the cracks.

We are a fully remote, diverse team spread across many time zones. Our shared value is simple: every customer deserves a great support experience.
